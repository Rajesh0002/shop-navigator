📱 How to Use Shop Navigator

1

Scan the QR Code

Scan the QR code displayed at the store entrance using your phone camera. This opens the store's navigation page — no app download needed!

2

Browse the Store Map

You'll see an interactive map showing all zones/aisles in the store. Each zone shows its name, icon, and number of products.

3

Search for Products

Use the search bar at the top to find any product. The store map will automatically highlight the zone where your product is located.

4

Filter by Category

Tap on a category (e.g., Fruits, Dairy, Snacks) to see all products in that category and their locations.

5

Locate a Product

Click "Locate" on any product to highlight its zone on the store map. The map scrolls to show you exactly where to go.

6

Build a Shopping List

Tap "Add to List" on products you want to buy. Your shopping list is organized by store zone for efficient shopping.

🛒 Shopping List Features

📋

Auto-Grouped by Zone

Your list is automatically sorted by store zone so you can shop aisle by aisle without backtracking.

✅

Mark as Found

Check off items as you pick them up. See your progress bar fill up as you shop.

💾

Auto-Saved

Your shopping list is saved in your browser. Close and reopen — your list is still there.

🧹

Clear Found Items

Once done, clear all found items with one tap. Remove individual items by clicking the X button.

🗺️ Store Map Guide

✨

Highlighted Zone

When you search or click "Locate", the matching zone lights up on the map.

👆

Click a Zone

Tap any zone to see all products available in that section.

🏷️

Product Count

Each zone shows how many products are in that area.

🎯

Offers Banner

Check the sliding offers banner at the top for current deals and discounts.

🔄 Smart Shopping Flows

Flow 1: Find a Specific Product

Search product → See highlighted zone → View details → Locate on map → Add to list

Flow 2: Browse by Category

Select category → See all products → Explore zones on map → Add items to list

Flow 3: Zone-by-Zone Shopping

Click zone on map → See all products → Add items → Move to next zone

Pro Tip: Build your shopping list first, then follow the zone-by-zone order to shop efficiently without going back and forth!

ℹ️ Store Information

Tap the info button (ℹ) in the header to see:

  • Store name and description
  • Phone number (tap to call directly)
  • Address with Google Maps directions link
  • Opening hours for each day of the week
  • Whether the store is currently open or closed

🚀 Getting Started as Shop Admin

1

Register Your Account

Go to the login page and register with your name, email, phone, and password.

2

Create Your Shop

From the dashboard, create a new shop with name, type (supermarket, textile, electronics, etc.), address, phone, and opening hours.

3

Add Zones (Store Layout)

Create zones representing aisles or sections in your store (e.g., "Fresh Produce", "Dairy", "Billing Counter"). Add icons, colors, and descriptions.

4

Add Categories

Create product categories (e.g., "Fruits & Vegetables", "Snacks", "Beverages") with icons and colors.

5

Add Products

Add products with name, price, description, photo, and assign them to a zone and category.

6

Generate QR Code

Generate the QR code for your shop and print it. Place it at your store entrance for customers to scan.

📊 Dashboard Features

🏪

Multiple Shops

Manage multiple stores from a single admin account. Each shop has its own QR code and products.

📦

Product Management

Add, edit, delete products. Update prices, stock status, photos, and zone assignments.

🗺️

Zone Management

Create and organize store zones/aisles. Each zone can have a photo, description, and grid position.

🎁

Offers & Deals

👥 Worker Management

1

Add Workers

Create worker accounts with name, email, password, and assign them to a specific shop.

2

Worker Permissions

Workers can manage products, zones, and categories for their assigned shop only. They cannot create/delete shops or manage other workers.

🔗 API Integration

Each shop gets a unique API key for external integrations:

  • Bulk import products via CSV
  • Update stock status from external systems
  • Sync product data with your POS system
  • Regenerate API key anytime from shop settings
Security: Never share your API key publicly. Regenerate it immediately if compromised.

👤 Worker Guide

As a worker, you can manage products and zones for your assigned shop.

1

Login

Use the email and password provided by your shop admin to log in.

2

Manage Products

Add new products, update prices, change stock status (in stock / out of stock), and upload product photos.

3

Manage Zones

Update zone names, photos, and descriptions. Rearrange products between zones.

4

Manage Categories & Offers

Add/edit categories and create promotional offers for your assigned shop.

Note: Workers can only access their assigned shop. You cannot create new shops, manage other workers, or regenerate API keys.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do customers need to download an app?
No! Customers just scan the QR code and the store navigation opens directly in their phone browser. No app download needed.
Is my shopping list saved?
Yes, your shopping list is automatically saved in your browser. It persists even if you close the page. Each store has its own separate list.
Can I manage multiple shops?
Yes! Admin accounts can create and manage multiple shops. Each shop gets its own QR code, products, zones, and worker assignments.
How do I update product prices?
Login as admin or worker → Go to Products → Click edit on any product → Update the price and save.
Can I import products in bulk?
Yes, use the API integration feature with your shop's API key to bulk import products via CSV or external systems.
